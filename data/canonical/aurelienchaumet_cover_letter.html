Job reference : People Analytics Analyst, Digital Workplace Engineering

In order to continue developing my skills, I am currently looking for a new job. I would like to nominate myself for the position of People Analytics Analyst within your team.

As Regional Observatory and GIS Project Manager at the Marennes Oléron Centre, my job today is to accompany thematic project managers, to understand their daily work and to propose digital tools (cartographic or analytical most of the time, but this can also concern productivity) which will facilitate their daily life.

My work is divided into 3 phases: the understanding of the needs of each user (whether in urban planning, on waste or economic development for example), transcribe them into technical solutions and train people in the use of the tools implemented, while recovering their improvement returns.

The quality of the data and the processes surrounding it, as well as the satisfaction of the end users, are part of my daily concerns.

I use Tableau very frequently, in particular to provide a collaborator with indicators and information on a complex geographical data (land use) or to present a portrait of territory during a meeting with elected officials.

I also had the opportunity to use Tableau in 2019, as an individual, by representing a large portion of the contributions at 30DayMapChallenge, held annually on Twitter. And it also allowed me to win the “Prix coup de coeur” du Hackaviz organized by the Grand Poitiers in 2019.

In addition to my professional skills in database management (PostgreSQL/ PostGIS) and data preparation (FME), since one year, I have started training in Python, particularly on data transformation and representation.

I also participate in the writing of a website about free tools of geomatics which named GeoTribu, by writing articles and newsletters

I discovered remote working last year during the COVID pandemic, and it was like a light just went off in my head. I really appreciate organizing job and personal life on this particular system. And I really think it will continue to develop over the world.
That’s why this job really interested me. I guess there are some keys for improve this kind of working organization. And as I’m always curious about new trends, I would be really excited to participate on discovering insights and sharing them.

Joining your company would be a great professional advancement for me. In addition to consolidating my professional skills, it would allow me to work directly on actions that have a direct impact on my personal beliefs.

I would appreciate the opportunity to meet with you to discuss how my qualifications will be beneficial to discover how we can all improve satisfaction and cooperation on remote working.
